About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student at KAIST under the supervision of Prof. Seunghwa Ryu, passionate about bridging AI and engineering. My work focuses on data-driven generative models for FEM, CFD, and multi-physics simulations, with the goal of automating design, simulation, and manufacturing processes through LLMs and digital twins.

Research

Deep Learning for Multi-Physics

Optimization × AI

Multi-Agent LLM Systems

Modeling fracture, thermo-fluids, and particle dynamics via numerics and AI.
